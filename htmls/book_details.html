The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck

Description of the Book:

This book offers a counterintuitive take on personal growth and happiness. Instead of promoting relentless positivity, Mark Manson argues that life’s struggles and limitations are inevitable and meaningful. He suggests that you should choose carefully what you “care about” — focusing only on the values and problems that truly matter to you — rather than trying to avoid discomfort or chase superficial success. Through a mix of personal anecdotes, humor, and practical insights, the book challenges typical self-help advice and encourages readers to embrace responsibility, accept life’s difficulties, and live with clarity about what’s truly important.

Category:

Nonfiction — specifically self-help / personal development. The book is often categorized under motivational and self-improvement genres.

Author:

Mark Manson — an American author, blogger, and personal-development writer known for his blunt, no-nonsense take on self-help and life philosophy.
