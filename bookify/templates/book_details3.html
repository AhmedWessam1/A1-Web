Atomic Habits

Category

Nonfiction — Self-Help / Personal Development. Focuses on habit formation, productivity, and long-term improvement.

Author

James Clear — an American author, speaker, and expert on habits, decision-making, and continuous improvement.

Published

First published in 2018.

Available

Description

Atomic Habits explains how small, consistent changes in behavior can lead to remarkable results over time. Instead of focusing on big goals, the book emphasizes building systems and improving daily habits.

James Clear introduces practical strategies for forming good habits, breaking bad ones, and mastering the tiny behaviors that lead to success. The book highlights how habits are shaped by identity and environment, and how small improvements compound into major achievements.

Key Lessons

  • Small habits, repeated daily, lead to big results over time.
  • Focus on systems, not just goals.
  • Habits are built through cues, cravings, responses, and rewards.
  • Make good habits obvious, attractive, easy, and satisfying.
  • Change your identity to change your behavior.
